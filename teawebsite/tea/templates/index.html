    Drink Tea!

    Tea by definition is "a hot drink made by infusing the dried, crushed leaves of the tea plant in boiling water". However, if you follow this definition and make all of your teas with boiling water, you will not get optimal results. Tea can have different flavors depending on how it is prepared and some teas, such as Green Tea, taste better if made with slightly cooler water. Then instead of being bitter, it will taste slightly sweet.

    There are many ways to enjoy tea with some combinations being more pleasant or complementary than others. I hope that by coming here you will find a way to enjoy tea in a way that suits your taste.
